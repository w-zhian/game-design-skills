# Design Constitution

跨 skill 共享的设计宪法。所有 skill 在产出设计方案时必须遵守这些原则和红线。

## Default Design Stance

- Player agency comes before accumulation. A reward is healthy only if it reinforces a meaningful choice.
- Systems should create readable consequences, not hidden spreadsheet pressure.
- Long-term games need repeatable tension: mastery, collection, social comparison, experimentation, or expression.
- Good complexity creates new decisions; bad complexity creates more labels for the same decision.
- A commercial design can be sharp without being hostile. Paid relief should not invalidate the free player's core play.

## Preferred Review Questions

- What is the player doing minute to minute?
- What decision becomes more interesting after 10 minutes, 10 hours, and 10 days?
- What can the player understand without a guide?
- What does the system make easier to tune later?
- What would a bored, optimal, or price-sensitive player do?

## Red Lines (HARD BLOCK — any design crossing these must be flagged)

- Fake choices where one option is always correct.
- Progression that only multiplies numbers without changing behavior.
- Required daily chores that do not create interesting planning.
- Currencies that exist only to obscure price or delay.
- Tutorials that explain UI before teaching why the player should care.
- Monetization pressure before the player has experienced the core loop's promise.
- Permanent uncrossable gaps between payers and non-payers in competitive modes.

## Personal Calibration (决明子)

These are confirmed preferences from prior project work:

- **数值优先于叙事**：先验证公式和产出消耗，再包装世界观
- **多品类交叉验证**：设计时习惯对照竞品（原神/鸣潮/星穹铁道/明日方舟）交叉验证
- **结构化交付**：偏好 Excel/Word 表格对比（改前vs改后两列），不用纯文字描述
- **配置规范**：Excel表头三行制（英文名/字段名/类型），禁用缩写
- **Unity C# 技术栈**：非 UE5，配置工具以 Excel 为主
- **强项品类**：塔防/卡牌/MMO/SLG/增量割草
